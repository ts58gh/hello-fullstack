Quick rules cheat-sheet
  • Bidding: each call must outrank the last. Strain order: ♣ < ♦ < ♥ < ♠ < NT.
  • Auction ends after 3 passes following any bid (or 4 passes from the start).
  • Contract: highest bid wins. Declarer is the first on that side to bid the contract's strain.
  • Play: opening lead from declarer's left. Dummy (declarer's partner) is revealed; declarer plays both hands.
  • Must follow suit if you can. Highest trump wins, else highest of led suit.
  • Make your contract or pay penalties. Game bonus when trick score ≥ 100.
